# Precision

> [!formula] Precision
> $$\text{Precision} = \frac{|\text{relevant} \cap \text{retrieved}|}{|\text{retrieved}|}$$
> 
> Fraction of retrieved documents that are relevant.

High precision = few false positives. See also [[Precision at K]], [[Recall]], [[F-Measure]].
